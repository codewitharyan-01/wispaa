The Concept

A quiet companion.

Designed to be felt, not seen. A minimalist pebble you wear close to your heart. It effortlessly hears your questions, connects to advanced AI, and whispers back the answers.

Micro-Acoustic Capture

Studio-grade dual MEMS microphones pick up your quietest whispers, perfectly isolating voice from wind.

Spatial Whispers

The precisely tuned upward-firing speaker delivers crisp, natural-sounding AI responses meant only for you.

Ambient Awareness LED

A tiny, soft light pulses elegantly to let you know exactly when Wispa is listening, thinking, or speaking.
